# ABOUT ME

Hi, I’m Roda :wave:. Thanks for dropping by! I’m from the Philippines :philippines: and currently based in New Zealand :new_zealand:. 

I’d describe myself as an inquisitive person :bulb: . Once something catches my interest, I tend to research deeply. And if it sparks a project, I make sure to see it through to completion :rocket:. I learn whether it succeeds or not: a working result gives me a useful tool, and a failure teaches me what to improve :tea:. Moving across the work cycle spectrum is better than being still.

That mindset is reflected in my diverse experience, including nursing :medical_symbol:, clinical administration :pen:, and office operations :wrench: within a health technology company.

I bring the same approach to my hobbies — sewing :sewing_needle:, embroidery, lettering :writing_hand:, and making miniatures — because they help me keep learning.

My strength is explaining complex ideas clearly and in a way that the target audience can relate to. I help teams stay aligned by translating technical concepts for colleagues and communicating them in everyday language to patients and clients.

I believe strong app design is based on real-world data structures  :globe_with_meridians:. It should perform reliably across operating systems, including older devices. 

Throughout my career, data integrity has been a constant priority, and it continues to guide my software development work, particularly in data management.
